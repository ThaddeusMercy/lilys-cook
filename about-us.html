ABOUT US
Lily's Cook ,is a boutique catering company that provides quality Nigerian and Afro Fusion food to match your style, budget and requirements. We believe food should not only taste great but should also look good and this reflects in everything we do.
We offer drop-off, pick-up and full catering service options for weddings, corporate functions,special events and private parties. Our goal is to provide our clients with a pleasant catering experience with convenient solutions that enhances their event and offers total peace of mind
At Lily's Cook , we adhere to NAFDAC guidelines to ensure food safety at all times and our environment is hygienic
MEET THE CEO
Majasan Mariam
Mariam founded Lily's Cook after identifying a cliche in the food industry. Mariam combines her passion for African and keen eye for detail to create a range of dishes that taste great with unique visual appeal.
Her innovative approach to cooking sees her exploring cooking technique and food styling methods from around the world adapting them to create a range of dishes that advances Nigerian food as we know it.
